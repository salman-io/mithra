About the College

Government Engineering College, Kozhikode (GECK) is a premier educational institution established by the Government of Kerala in 1999. The college is dedicated to imparting quality technical education and fostering research and development. It is affiliated with APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and approved by AICTE. GECK strives to create globally competent engineers who are socially committed and ethically responsible.
